Welcome to the S-Parameters Masterclass

This comprehensive masterclass will teach you how to read and interpret S-parameters like a book. S-parameters are the medium of exchange of information that describe everything important about the electrical performance of interconnects. Whether you're working with measurements, simulations, or system performance analysis, understanding S-parameters is crucial for signal integrity engineering.

Throughout this course, you'll learn to identify patterns in S-parameters and understand what they tell you about the behavior of interconnects. Rather than focusing on mathematical formalism, we'll explore what the math tells us through practical examples using both virtual and real prototypes.

About the Instructor

Dr. Eric Bogatin

Eric Bogatin is a physicist with more than 40 years of experience in the electronics industry, working in various large companies, small companies, and tool vendors. About 30 years ago, he started a company with his wife to offer training and education services in the field of signal integrity.

Currently, Dr. Bogatin is on the faculty at the University of Colorado in Boulder, where he teaches undergraduate courses and graduate courses in signal integrity. He is also the technical editor of the Signal Integrity Journal.

S-parameters are one of the topics he spends considerable time teaching engineers about, as they are critically important for all signal integrity professionals.
